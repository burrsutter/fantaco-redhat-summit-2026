# FantaCo Return Policy

## Standard Returns

Customers may return any unopened FantaCo product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. The original receipt or order confirmation email is required for all returns. Products must be in their original packaging and in resalable condition.

## Defective Products

Defective products may be returned within 90 days of purchase for a full refund or replacement. A product is considered defective if it arrives damaged, has manufacturing defects, or does not match the product description. Customers do not need original packaging for defective returns, but must provide proof of purchase.

## Office Furniture & Large Items

Office furniture, standing desks, chairs, and other large items must be inspected upon delivery. Damage or defects must be reported within 14 days. Returns of assembled furniture require disassembly by the customer unless the return is due to a FantaCo error. FantaCo will arrange freight pickup for approved large-item returns at no charge.

## Imagination Pod Services

Design, construction, and installation services for Imagination Pod projects are non-refundable once work has commenced. Customers may cancel an approved project before the construction phase begins and receive a refund minus a 10% design-recovery fee. Fixture and technology components installed as part of a pod project follow the standard 90-day defective-product policy. Disputes related to completed pod work should be raised through your assigned sales representative.

## Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned product. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a FantaCo error or defective product.

## Bulk Order Returns

For orders exceeding $500 or 100 units, returns must be coordinated with the FantaCo Sales team directly. A restocking fee of 15% applies to bulk returns of non-defective products. Contact your assigned sales representative or email bulk-returns@fantaco.com.
